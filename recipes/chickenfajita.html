Chicken Fajita

Description

a quick and easy Chicken Fajita Recipe for all the family!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • chicken breast chopped
  • Onion
  • Mixed peppers
  • salsa
  • Fajita seasoning mix
  • tortillas

Steps

  1. Finely chop onions
  2. add to pan with oil and chopped peppers
  3. Cook until softened
  4. add chicken to pan and cook until golden
  5. add fajita mix and coat chicken
  6. cook for 15 minutes
  7. add to wrap with salsa
  8. serve
