Lasagna

Description

Prepare this easy homemade lasagna ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Just bake for an extra 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • prosciutto
  • Passata
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Lean Beef Mince
  • Beef Stock
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • White Sauce
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. heat 2 tbsp olive oil and cook lean beef mince until brown
  2. finely chop slices of prosciutto - stir through meat mixture
  3. pour passata beef stock and season
  4. heat oven
  5. spoon 1/3 of meat sauce into dish cover with lasagne sheet drizzle with white sauce
  6. repeat 3x
  7. shred mozzarella over top
  8. bake for 45 mins until lightly browned
  9. serve
