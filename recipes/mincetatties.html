Mince & Tatties

Description

If you’ve grown up in Scotland, there is a 99.99% chance your family will have a Mince & Tatties recipe. But there are a few different variations in how it’s made, dependant on who makes it.


Ingredients

  • Olive/Vegetable Oil
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Mixed Herbs
  • 20% Beef Mince
  • Beef OXO
  • worcestershire Sauce
  • Gravy Granules
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop carrots and onions
  2. add to pan with oil and mixed herbs
  3. Cook until softened
  4. add beef mince to pot and season with salt and pepper
  5. Cook mince until browned
  6. add worcestershire sauce and beef stock cube dissolved in water
  7. Simmer for approx 30 minutes
  8. Boil Potatoes
  9. serve
